About Fresh People

Over a decade of excellence in event staffing across South Africa

Our Story

Founded in Johannesburg, Fresh People has grown from a small team of event professionals to one of South Africa's premier event staffing agencies. Over the past 10+ years, we've provided exceptional staff for hundreds of corporate events, brand activations, and special occasions.

Our commitment to excellence, professionalism, and attention to detail has earned us the trust of leading brands and event planners across the country.
